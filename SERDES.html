About SERDES

Serializer/Desearlizer utilized for data transmission by converting parallel data to sequential data to minimize the usage of wires

Working on SERDES


I developed and understood the concepts of SERDES, I understood schematics for linear and non-linear CDR.


I utilized VHDL to describe modules to be implemented.


I developed and implemented a Bang Bang Phase detector utilizing MRCP architecture, achieving 3X oversampling. A phase generator, phase rotator, clock divider and a digital filter were described in VHDL as part of the MRCP architecture and combined appropriately to the schematics provided.


I utilized simulink to conduct tests and view eye diagrams of the circuit for optimal performance. Quartus Prime II was utilized to describe hardware on VHDL and compiled onto an Altera FPGA.


I wrote and submitted weekly reports on project progress to key stakeholders updating them on the project and key milestones.
